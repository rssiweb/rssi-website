What is RSSI?

RSSI is a registered, non-governmental organization, a non-profit and voluntary citizens' group which is organized on a local level.

Our mission is to empower underprivileged children, youth and their families and community through relevant education, innovative healthcare and market-focused livelihood programmes. Mutual respect, trust & honesty are the three pillars of our institute and we are thoroughly committed to them.

How old is RSSI?

Rina Shiksha Sahayak Foundation came into existence in the year 2010, founded by Rina Saha. At first, it started functioning from West Bengal and later in 2015 shifted to Gomti Nagar, Lucknow.

Which areas does RSSI work in?

RSSI has been working in the thematic areas of education, healthcare, youth employability and empowerment of women and girls.
