HISTORY

Empowering youth through education is the main focus of our organization. Mission Education is a regional level program of RSSI, Mumbai (mainly covering Thane area including Ghodbunder Rd, Bhayandarpada) which is committed to ensuring the basic education and healthcare of underprivileged children.

We believe that whether you are addressing healthcare, poverty, population control, unemployment or human rights, there's no better place to start than in the corridors of education.
