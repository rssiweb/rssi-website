    Group Internship Information
    Important for Group Applications

    We welcome group internship applications for college projects, but please note:

    • All students must apply individually through our registration portal
    • Each intern will receive their own individual internship certificate
    • For group submissions to your college, you can merge individual certificates
    • Group coordination should be managed by the students
    • Project topics and requirements should align with your college guidelines

    Note: Combined group certificates are not issued. Each student receives individual certification.

    Application Process
    1
    Online Registration

    Visit the official website of RSSI NGO. Navigate to the Internships portal and register yourself by selecting the type of association as "Internship".

    2
    Identity Verification

    After registration, you will receive an email with your candidate profile credentials and a secure identity verification link.

    Log in to your profile and upload the required documents for verification. Only Aadhaar Card is accepted for identity verification.

    Processing Time: Interview schedule within 7-14 business days after identity verification completion.
    3
    Interview & Selection

    Upon successful verification, you'll receive an interview schedule. The selection process includes:

    • Document Verification: Visit RSSI centre with original documents
    • Personal Interview: Assessment of skills, interest, and suitability
    • Internship Discussion: Alignment of goals and expectations
    Required Documents
    Mandatory for All
    Aadhaar Card (original)
    High School Marksheet (10th)
    Intermediate Marksheet (12th)
    Updated Resume/CV
    For College Students
    College ID Card (Current)
    Latest Marksheet/Transcript (showing current year)
    College Internship Letter (if required)

    Important: College students must bring their College ID Card and latest marksheet as proof of current year of study. Graduates should bring their Graduation Certificate instead.

    Document Verification Rules:
    • Only original documents or live DigiLocker certificates accepted
    • Screenshots, photocopies, or scanned copies will not be processed
    • All documents must be current, valid, and legible
