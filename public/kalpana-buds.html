About Kalpana Buds School

A Division of Rina Shiksha Sahayak Foundation

Kalpana Buds School is dedicated to providing exceptional early childhood education for children from Nursery to Upper Kindergarten (UKG). Our philosophy centers on nurturing the whole child - emotionally, socially, intellectually, and physically.

We believe that the early years are critical for laying the foundation for lifelong learning. Our child-centric approach combines structured learning with ample playtime, ensuring that children develop essential skills while having fun.

Our safe, vibrant, and stimulating environment encourages exploration, creativity, and the joy of discovery. We're proud to be part of the Rina Shiksha Sahayak Foundation, continuing a legacy of educational excellence.

Our Programs

Age-appropriate curriculum designed for holistic development

Nursery

Age: 2.5 - 3.5 Years

Our Nursery program focuses on socialization, language development, and basic motor skills through engaging play activities.

  • Sensory play & exploration
  • Basic shapes & colors
  • Storytelling & rhymes
  • Fine motor skill development

LKG

Age: 3.5 - 4.5 Years

LKG program introduces structured learning with a focus on language, numbers, and creative expression through various activities.

  • Phonics & pre-writing skills
  • Number concepts 1-20
  • Creative arts & crafts
  • Social skills development

UKG

Age: 4.5 - 5.5 Years

UKG prepares children for formal schooling with enhanced focus on literacy, numeracy, and overall cognitive development.

  • Reading readiness & writing
  • Mathematical concepts
  • Science exploration
  • Problem-solving activities
