Learn More About Us

Rina Shiksha Sahayak Foundation (RSSI) is a pioneering learning organization committed to transforming education in India. Founded in 2010 with a focus on uplifting children in Uttar Pradesh's slums, our reach has expanded significantly across the nation.

We address educational disparities through innovative, scalable, and affordable interventions. Our work spans the entire age spectrum, from early childhood to youth. Through strategic partnerships with universities, corporations, and community organizations, we empower millions of children and prevent thousands from dropping out of school each year.

Our flagship program, Shiksha, offers a holistic approach to child development, encompassing basic education, vocational skills, healthcare, and extracurricular activities. Swabhiman and Suraksha are dedicated to empowering women and adolescent girls with essential healthcare and hygiene knowledge.

Kalpana Buds School, launched in the academic year 2024-25, is RSSI's new morning division. This school is a testament to our commitment to quality education. It provides a nurturing environment for young learners, focusing on holistic development and preparing them for a bright future.

Why Choose RSSI?

RSSI is a registered, non-governmental organization, a non-profit and voluntary citizens' group which is organized at the national level.

RSSI facilitates the underprivileged youth by setting up mini-libraries, computer training centres, vocational training centres and awareness programs for skill development schemes, which make them more skilled and employable.

Major Projects

Shiksha

Shiksha is a self-funded initiative by RSSI NGO that delivers quality education at a fraction of the market cost. At Kalpana Buds School, students contribute only around 13% of the actual educational expenses. In our NGO division, students pay less than 4% of the market cost. The majority of the expenses are covered by our directorial fund, making education both affordable and empowering for the students. By bridging this financial gap, we ensure that every child has the opportunity to thrive. Learn more about how you can support our work.

Suraksha

For the promotion of menstrual hygiene among adolescent girls aged 10-19 in rural areas, we focus on improving access to hygienic sanitary products and educating girls about the menstrual cycle. Our comprehensive approach includes regular health screenings, monthly awareness sessions, and the free distribution of sanitary pads as needed. This holistic strategy ensures that girls are well-informed, prepared, and supported throughout their menstrual health journey.

Swabhiman

Launched in 2017 to address the challenges through a simple yet effective approach. The programme is specifically aimed at realization of both individual and collective self-esteem and inner strength for marginalized and socially excluded women and adolescent girls through innovative community practices.
