Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Our Commitment to Privacy

Baangee Traders is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services:

Information Type Examples Purpose of Collection
Personal Information Name, email, phone number, address Account creation, order processing, communication
Business Information Company name, GST number, business address Corporate services, invoicing, compliance
Financial Information Payment details, transaction history Payment processing, financial records
Technical Information IP address, browser type, device information Website analytics, security, improvement
Usage Information Pages visited, services used, preferences Service improvement, personalization

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To process your orders and transactions
  • To communicate with you about orders, services, and promotions
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect against fraudulent activities
  • To personalize your experience

3. Data Protection & Security

Security Measures

Secure data transmission using 256-bit SSL encryption

Secure Storage

Encrypted databases with access controls

Access Control

Restricted access to personal data

Regular Audits

Regular security assessments and updates

4. Your Privacy Rights

Right to Access

You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Right to Correction

You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

Right to Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data under certain conditions.

Right to Object

You can object to the processing of your personal data.

Right to Portability

You can request transfer of your data to another organization.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Marketing Cookies: Track effectiveness of marketing campaigns

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Third-Party Sharing

We may share your information with trusted third parties in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, delivery services, IT support
  • Business Partners: For joint services or promotions
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers or acquisitions

We ensure that all third parties maintain appropriate security measures and use data only for specified purposes.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary:

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Transaction Records: 7 years for financial and tax purposes
  • Inactive Accounts: Data deleted after 2 years of inactivity
  • Marketing Data: Until consent is withdrawn

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

As an Indian company, we primarily process data within India. However, some service providers may be located outside India. In such cases, we ensure adequate data protection measures are in place.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
