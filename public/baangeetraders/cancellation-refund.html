Cancellation & Refund Policy

Clear guidelines for order cancellations and refunds across all our services

Policy Overview

This policy outlines the terms for cancellations and refunds for Baangee Traders services. Please read carefully as policies vary by service type.

Service-Specific Policies

Corporate Voucher Solutions

Cancellation Policy

Voucher orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of purchase. After 24 hours, cancellations may be subject to processing fees. Activated vouchers cannot be cancelled.

Refund Policy

Refunds for cancelled voucher orders will be processed within 7-10 business days. Refund amount may be reduced by processing fees and any third-party charges.

Validity & Expiry

Vouchers have specific validity periods. Expired vouchers cannot be refunded or extended. Please check validity before purchase.

CSC Educational Services

Library Registration

Library membership fees are non-refundable. Cancellations must be submitted 7 days before the next billing cycle.

Book Orders

Books can be returned within 7 days if damaged or incorrect. Returns must be in original condition with all accessories.

Educational Programs

Program fees are refundable if cancelled at least 15 days before program start date, minus administrative charges.

Agriculture Products

Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled before dispatch. Once dispatched, cancellations are not possible. Contact us immediately for cancellation requests.

Returns & Refunds

Defective or damaged products can be returned within 15 days of delivery. Returns require proof of purchase and original packaging.

Quality Issues

For quality complaints, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos/videos as evidence. Refunds or replacements will be processed after investigation.

Construction Materials

Cancellation Window

Cancellations accepted within 6 hours of order placement for standard items. Custom orders cannot be cancelled once production begins.

Returns Policy

Materials can be returned within 7 days if unopened and in original condition. Bulk materials (cement, aggregates) cannot be returned.

Defective Products

Defective materials must be reported within 24 hours of delivery. We will arrange inspection and replacement if defect is verified.
