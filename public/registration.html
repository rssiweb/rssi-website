You need to register your profile in the RSSI talent pool. The registration process consists of three parts.

Part I - Registration and Payment

Familiarize yourself with the information required of a potential volunteer/intern/employee and submit basic information by clicking on the Join Now button from the job description section to create your account.

Complete the payment by clicking on the Application fee section.

Exclusively applicable to job applicants and NOT applicable for Project/Internship programs.

Application fee Rs.500 is one-time, and has to be paid at the time of registration.

Notes

  1. If a candidate is not selected by RSSI NGO during the selection process, they will receive a full refund of their application fee.
  2. If a candidate is selected and joins the organization, they will receive a refund of Rs.400/- after completion of 1 month.
  3. However, if a candidate is selected but does not join the organization, no refund will be processed.

Part II - Identity verification

After completing Part 1, in order for us to be able to process your application, please click on the link provided in the confirmation email to proceed with the identity verification process. Acceptable identification

  1. Aadhaar
  2. Current passport
  3. Driving license
  4. Electoral identity card
  5. Ration card
Part III - Selection process

  1. Wait for the NGO to share interview slots with you.
  2. Prepare for the interview by researching about RSSI NGO, their mission, and the specific program you're applying for.
  3. Attend the scheduled interview and perform your best.
  4. Wait for the interview results. If selected, follow instructions provided in the email to update your profile.
  5. If a candidate is not selected by RSSI NGO during the selection process, they will receive a full refund of their application fee. Refunds will be issued within 30 days of the candidate's non-selection.
Part IV - Onboarding process

  1. Upon updating your profile, an offer letter will be generated. Kindly confirm your acceptance of the offer by responding to the letter.
  2. Once you have accepted the offer, you will be sent a formal joining letter containing all the necessary instructions for the onboarding process.
  3. Ensure the completion of all prerequisites required for the onboarding process.
  4. On the designated date and time, please visit the specified center to initiate your onboarding process and integrate into the system.
  5. Upon successfully completing the first month, the candidate (job applicants) will be eligible for a Rs. 400/- refund.
Offline support

If you are not tech-savvy and want to avail offline facility to complete the application process, please send us a WhatsApp message on this number 7980168159 along with one of your government photo ID proofs. Please note that offline facility is available with an additional service charge of Rs.150.
