    Job Summary

    We are seeking dedicated and passionate Pre-Primary and Primary Teachers to join our educational team. The ideal candidates will be responsible for creating a nurturing, engaging, and stimulating learning environment that supports the academic, social, and emotional development of young learners.

    The teachers will independently plan and deliver age-appropriate lessons, implement the curriculum effectively, assess student progress, and maintain a safe, positive, and inclusive classroom environment while collaborating with colleagues and parents.

    Key Responsibilities
    • Plan and deliver age-appropriate lessons and learning activities for pre-primary and primary students in line with the prescribed curriculum
    • Create a nurturing, engaging, and inclusive classroom environment that supports students’ academic, social, emotional, and cognitive development
    • Assess and monitor students’ progress, maintaining accurate records and providing constructive feedback
    • Maintain a safe, clean, and well-organized classroom environment conducive to effective learning
    • Manage classroom behavior positively, ensuring adherence to school policies and guidelines
    • Supervise students during classroom activities, transitions, and outdoor playtime
    • Collaborate with colleagues and school administration to support academic programs and school initiatives
    • Communicate regularly and professionally with parents or guardians regarding students’ progress and development
    • Participate in training programs, meetings, and professional development activities to enhance teaching effectiveness
    Qualifications & Requirements
    • Bachelor's degree (Early Childhood Education or related field preferred)
    • Previous experience working with young children in a childcare, preschool, or educational setting is desirable
    • Understanding of child development principles and age-appropriate teaching methodologies
    • Strong communication and interpersonal skills to interact effectively with students, colleagues, and parents
    • Patience, empathy, and a genuine passion for working with young children
    • Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment and follow instructions from lead teachers and supervisors
    • Basic knowledge of classroom technology and educational software/tools is a plus
    • CPR/First Aid certification (or willingness to obtain upon hire)

    Application Process
    Step 1: Online Registration

    Visit the official website of RSSI NGO. Navigate to the Careers portal and register yourself as an employee by selecting the type of association as Employee and using the job code "FT-PTA".

    Step 2: Identity Verification

    After completing the registration process, you will receive an email for identity verification. This email will contain your candidate profile credentials along with a secure identity verification link.

    Please log in to your profile using the provided credentials and upload the required documents listed below for identity verification. Once submitted, kindly wait for confirmation from RSSI NGO.

    In case the verification is rejected, you will be notified by email and will be able to re-upload the required documents through your candidate portal. If the verification is successful, your application will proceed to the next stage of the selection process.

    Please note that only the following form of identification is accepted:

    • Aadhaar Card
    Step 3: Interview Process

    Upon completion of the verification process, the candidate will receive the interview schedule.

    The selection process will be conducted in the following order: document verification, eligibility test, demo class, and interview. Candidates are required to visit the RSSI Centre for verification and a 30-minute eligibility test covering Language and Subject Knowledge, Pedagogy, and General Aptitude.

    All original documents must be brought for verification and will be returned to the candidate after completion of the process. If original documents are not available, candidates must present live certificates directly from the DigiLocker mobile application to the verifying officer. Photocopies or screenshots from DigiLocker will not be accepted.

    Candidates who successfully clear the document verification and eligibility test will proceed to a 45-minute demo class, followed by the interview. The demo class topic will be shared in advance.

    Step 4: HR Interview

    Candidates who successfully clear the previous stages will be invited for an online HR interview. The interview will be conducted virtually, and a meeting invitation with the date and time will be shared in advance via email.

    Step 5: Selection & Onboarding

    If selected, you will receive an offer letter. Upon acceptance, a formal joining letter containing onboarding instructions will be issued.
