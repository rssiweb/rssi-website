About RSSI Foundation

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Rina Shiksha Sahayak Foundation (RSSI) is a registered nonprofit working to provide quality education, healthcare, and empowerment programs to underserved communities across India.

Our Core Initiatives

Holistic programs addressing multiple dimensions of development

Shiksha Initiative

Comprehensive education programs focusing on:

  • Quality primary education
  • Vocational skill development
  • Holistic child development
  • Digital literacy programs

Suraksha Initiative

Health and hygiene programs including:

  • Menstrual hygiene awareness
  • Sanitary pad distribution (300+ in 2024)
  • Health camps (150+ beneficiaries)
  • Breaking taboos around menstruation

Swabhiman Initiative

Women empowerment programs focusing on:

  • Self-reliance training
  • Healthcare access
  • Skill development
  • Mitigating domestic violence impacts

Kalpana Buds School

Nurturing early potential through affordable, quality education

  • Play-based learning approach
  • Holistic development focus
  • 102 current students (50% girls)
  • Nominal fees (~13% of actual cost)
