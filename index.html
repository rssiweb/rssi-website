Empowering youth through education is the main focus of our organization.

Mission Education is a regional level program of R.S Success Institute, Mumbai (mainly covering Thane area including Ghodbunder Rd, Bhayandarpada) which is committed to ensuring the basic education and healthcare of underprivileged children.

We believe that whether you are addressing healthcare, poverty, population control, unemployment or human rights, there's no better place to start than in the corridors of education.

500+ Beneficiaries

In past 2 years we have covered almost 500+ beneficiaries under our educational and health care program. Health care program covers Feminine hygiene, basic personal hygiene for all.

25+ Volunteers

Our journey started with 2 volunteers and gradually connected with more than 25 volunteers across the world.

3 Centre

Our NGO division is now active in Mumbai. Mumbai centre of RSSI is a non-governmental organization, a non-profit and voluntary citizens' group which is organized on a local level.

What we did so far?


  • Mission Education: A regional level program on education, health care and overall development. Mainly covering Gomti Nagar area including Vibhuti Khand, Vishesh Khand, Vikrant Khand, and Vijayanti Khand.

      1. Free regular classes (up to Intermediate +2).
      2. Educational aid up to ₹ 12,000 under Swabhiman Prakalpa.
      3. Blanket, educational stationary, cycle distribution under donation drive.
      4. Outdoor educational tour at Nawab Wajid Ali Shah Zoological Garden and Lucknow Metro.
      5. Cultural celebration

  • Awareness, sensitization on child sexual abuse.

  • Menstrual Hygiene sensitization and donation of Sanitary Pads to underprivileged girls and women.

  • Tree plantation, Swachh Bharat Mission and generic personal hygiene drive.
