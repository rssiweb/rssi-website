RSSI Teachers' Training Program-Batch 3

In order for us to be able to process your application, you are requested to complete the steps given below.

Part I/Registration

Please fill out this form with detailed information to complete your registration. Upon successful submission of the form, you will receive a system-generated registration ID that will uniquely identify you throughout the program.

Registration Form
Part II/Course Fee submission

Once you have received your Unique Registration ID, please complete your payment using the URL provided below. Either you can make the UPI payment by scanning the QR code displayed in the portal or you can follow the traditional way of transferring money using bank account details.

After a successful transaction, fill the payment details form with the required information, i.e., Registration Number (which you will receive after registration), Registered Email ID and Transaction ID. Do not share Transaction ID with others.


Now your application will be in HR queue. After HR approval, you will receive two emails – first, confirmation of your payment (within 2 business days), second, your login credentials and information related to the induction session.

Your profile will be considered for upcoming assignments only after the completion of both parts of the registration process.
