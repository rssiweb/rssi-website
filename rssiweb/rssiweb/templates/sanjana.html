Now you might came up with a question that why should we listen music? What are it's benefits?

😄 Let me now explain it:-

  1. Improves mood:- Studies show that listening to music can benefit overall well-being, help regulate emotions, and create happiness and relaxation in everyday life.
  2. Reduces stress:- Listening to ‘relaxing’ music which have a slow tempo, low pitch, and no lyrics has been shown to reduce stress.
  3. Improves memory:- If you are studying and aren't able to remember things, being comfused.Listening to music it will help you experience more,have a better memorising things, less confusion, and better focused attention.
  4. Eases pain:-In studies of patients recovering from surgery, those who listened to music before, during, or after surgery had less pain and more overall satisfaction compared with patients who did not listen to music as part of their care.

And many more.But these are some major benefits.
