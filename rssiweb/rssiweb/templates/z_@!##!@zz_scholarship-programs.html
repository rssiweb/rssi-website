Empowering Girls Through Education.

RSSI is an educational center in India whose vision is to contribute to the empowerment of Indian women by supporting academically outstanding and financially deserving girls, through scholarship to pursue higher education.

RSSI supports girls from dropout until senior secondary level. RSSI scholarships are merit-cum-need based scholarship. We have three categories here, Swabhiman Prakalpa, Foundation and Post Matric scheme.


Swabhiman Prakalpa

Only for the underprivileged girl. We also cover dropout girls (age group of 10 to 20 years) under this category.

 View

Foundation Scheme

It covers both primary and secondary level. To be eligible for this category candidates have to clear RSSI scholarship test without any consideration and annual income of their parents/guardian from all sources needs to be under ₹ 1 lakh.

 View

Post Matric Scheme

Applicable for senior secondary level students. Candidates who have secured above 69% marks or equivalent grade in the board examination and the annual income of parents/guardian from all sources does not exceed ₹ 2 lakh are eligible for this category.
