How do I join the Shiksha Project as a student?


You are just one step away to start a better future with RSSI. Please fill the form carefully and you are done. Soon after filling the form, someone from our team will contact you and will tell you about the next steps.

Please keep the below mentioned document with you before you start filling the form. If you are not tech-savvy and want to avail offline facility, please visit the center with the documents mentioned here.

    1. 1 photocopy / soft copy of your Aadhaar card

    2. 1 soft copy of your current formal photo
